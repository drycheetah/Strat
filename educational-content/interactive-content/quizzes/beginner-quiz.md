## Test Your Knowledge

### Quiz 1: Blockchain Basics (10 Questions)

**Question 1**: What is a blockchain?
- A) A type of cryptocurrency
- B) A distributed ledger technology
- C) A computer program
- D) A website

**Answer**: B
**Explanation**: A blockchain is a distributed ledger that records transactions across multiple computers.

---

**Question 2**: What makes blockchain transactions immutable?
- A) Encryption
- B) Cryptographic linking of blocks
- C) Cloud storage
- D) Government regulation

**Answer**: B
**Explanation**: Each block contains a hash of the previous block, creating an unbreakable chain.

---

**Question 3**: What is a private key?
- A) Your username
- B) Your wallet address
- C) A secret code that controls your assets
- D) A password for exchanges

**Answer**: C
**Explanation**: Your private key is the cryptographic secret that gives you control over your blockchain assets.

---

**Question 4**: What happens if you lose your private key?
- A) You can reset it with your email
- B) The exchange can recover it
- C) Your funds are permanently lost
- D) STRAT support can help

**Answer**: C
**Explanation**: Blockchain is decentralized - if you lose your private key, no one can recover your funds.

---

**Question 5**: What is gas in blockchain?
- A) Fuel for cars
- B) A type of cryptocurrency
- C) A fee for processing transactions
- D) A mining reward

**Answer**: C
**Explanation**: Gas is the fee paid to validators/miners for processing your transaction.

---

**Question 6**: What does DeFi stand for?
- A) Defined Finance
- B) Decentralized Finance
- C) Digital Finance
- D) Deferred Finance

**Answer**: B
**Explanation**: DeFi means Decentralized Finance - financial services without intermediaries.

---

**Question 7**: What is staking?
- A) Gambling with cryptocurrency
- B) Locking tokens to secure the network
- C) Trading on exchanges
- D) Mining with hardware

**Answer**: B
**Explanation**: Staking involves locking your tokens to help secure the network and earn rewards.

---

**Question 8**: What is an NFT?
- A) New Financial Transaction
- B) Non-Fungible Token
- C) Network Fee Token
- D) Negotiable Fund Transfer

**Answer**: B
**Explanation**: NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token - a unique digital asset.

---

**Question 9**: What is the purpose of a block explorer?
- A) To mine blocks
- B) To view blockchain data
- C) To create wallets
- D) To buy cryptocurrency

**Answer**: B
**Explanation**: Block explorers let you view transactions, addresses, and blocks on the blockchain.

---

**Question 10**: What is a DAO?
- A) Digital Asset Owner
- B) Decentralized Autonomous Organization
- C) Data Access Object
- D) Distributed Application Order

**Answer**: B
**Explanation**: DAO stands for Decentralized Autonomous Organization - a community-governed entity.

## Scenario-Based Questions (5 Scenarios)

### Scenario 1
You receive an email saying you won 1,000 STRAT, but need to send your private key to claim it. What do you do?

**Answer**: This is a scam. Never share your private key. Legitimate giveaways never ask for keys.

---

### Scenario 2
You're sending 500 STRAT to a friend. The transaction is stuck for 10 minutes. What should you check?

**Answer**: Check gas price (may be too low), network congestion, and verify on block explorer. Consider increasing gas or waiting.

---

### Scenario 3
You found a DeFi protocol offering 1000% APY. Should you invest all your STRAT?

**Answer**: No! Extremely high APY is often unsustainable and may indicate a scam. Do research, check audits, and never invest more than you can afford to lose.

---

### Scenario 4
Your hardware wallet is damaged. How do you recover your funds?

**Answer**: Use your seed phrase to recover your wallet on a new device. This is why backup is critical.

---

### Scenario 5
You want to participate in STRAT governance. What do you need?

**Answer**: STRAT tokens (1 STRAT = 1 vote), understanding of proposals, and active participation in community discussions.
