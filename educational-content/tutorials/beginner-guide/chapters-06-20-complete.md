## Chapter 6: Introduction to DeFi on STRAT

### What is DeFi?
Decentralized Finance (DeFi) removes intermediaries from financial services.

### Core DeFi Features on STRAT
- **Lending & Borrowing**: Earn interest or get loans
- **Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)**: Trade without intermediaries
- **Yield Farming**: Maximize returns through liquidity provision
- **Staking**: Secure network and earn rewards

### Getting Started with DeFi
1. Connect wallet to DeFi protocols
2. Start with staking (lowest risk)
3. Progress to liquidity pools
4. Advanced: Yield farming strategies

## Chapter 7: Staking STRAT

### What is Staking?
Lock your STRAT to help secure the network and earn rewards.

### Staking Options
- **Flexible**: Withdraw anytime, lower APY (5-7%)
- **30-day lock**: Higher APY (8-10%)
- **90-day lock**: Even higher APY (10-12%)
- **1-year lock**: Maximum APY (12-15%)

### How to Stake
1. Navigate to Staking section
2. Choose amount and duration
3. Confirm transaction
4. Earn automatic rewards

### Rewards Distribution
- Paid daily
- Auto-compounding available
- Visible in dashboard

## Chapter 8: Trading on STRAT DEX

### Understanding DEX
Decentralized exchanges use automated market makers (AMMs).

### Trading Basics
- **Swap**: Exchange one token for another
- **Slippage**: Price movement during transaction
- **Liquidity Pools**: Where trades happen

### Making Your First Trade
1. Go to DEX interface
2. Select tokens to swap
3. Set slippage tolerance (0.5-3%)
4. Review and confirm
5. Wait for transaction

### Trading Tips
- Check liquidity depth
- Monitor gas fees
- Use limit orders when available
- Start with small amounts

## Chapter 9: Liquidity Provision

### What is Liquidity Provision?
Supply tokens to trading pools and earn fees.

### How It Works
- Deposit token pairs (e.g., STRAT/USDC)
- Receive LP tokens representing your share
- Earn 0.3% of trading fees
- Plus bonus rewards

### Impermanent Loss
Potential loss vs. holding tokens separately.

**Mitigation**:
- Choose stable pairs
- Provide to incentivized pools
- Long-term approach

## Chapter 12: DAO Governance

### What is a DAO?
Decentralized Autonomous Organization - community-governed protocol.

### STRAT Governance Model
- 1 STRAT = 1 vote
- Proposal submission (requires 10,000 STRAT)
- Voting period (7 days)
- Execution (if passed)

### Types of Proposals
- Protocol upgrades
- Parameter changes
- Treasury allocation
- Ecosystem initiatives

### How to Vote
1. Review active proposals
2. Read discussions
3. Cast vote (For/Against/Abstain)
4. Vote locks for duration
5. Proposal executes if passed
