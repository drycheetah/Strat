STAKING QUICK GUIDE
================================================================================

Lock Periods:
  ‚Ä¢ short    (1,000 blocks / ~1.9 days)  - 3% APY
  ‚Ä¢ medium   (5,000 blocks / ~9.5 days)  - 5% APY
  ‚Ä¢ long     (10,000 blocks / ~19 days)  - 8% APY
  ‚Ä¢ extended (25,000 blocks / ~47.6 days) - 12% APY

Minimum stake: 1 STRAT

How it works:
  1. Create stake with lockPeriod
  2. Wait for unlock block
  3. Claim rewards (optional - doesn't unlock principal)
  4. Unlock when lock period complete
  5. Withdraw principal + rewards
