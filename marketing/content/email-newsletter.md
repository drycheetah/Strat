## Newsletter Metrics to Track

**Engagement**:
- Open rate (target: 35%+)
- Click-through rate (target: 5%+)
- Reply rate
- Forward/share rate

**Growth**:
- New subscribers per week
- Referral signups
- Unsubscribe rate (keep <2%)
- List growth rate

**Content Performance**:
- Most clicked links
- Most engaged sections
- Device breakdown (mobile vs desktop)
- Time of day opened

**Tools**:
- Mailchimp Analytics
- Google Analytics (for website traffic from newsletter)
- UTM parameters for link tracking

## A/B Testing Ideas

**Subject Lines**:
- Emoji vs no emoji
- Question vs statement
- Urgency vs informational
- Personalization vs generic

**Send Times**:
- Friday 10 AM vs Thursday 2 PM
- Weekend vs weekday
- Different time zones

**Content Format**:
- Long-form vs short updates
- Text-heavy vs image-heavy
- Formal vs casual tone

**CTAs**:
- Button color and text
- Single CTA vs multiple
- Placement (top vs bottom)
