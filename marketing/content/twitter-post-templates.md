- 7-9 AM: Catch morning traders
- 11 AM-1 PM: Lunch hour browsing
- 6-8 PM: Evening engagement
- 10-11 PM: Asia waking up

### Daily Posting Schedule
- **Morning**: Market update or GM
- **Midday**: Educational or technical
- **Afternoon**: Community engagement
- **Evening**: Announcement or milestone
- **Night**: Fun/meme content

### Hashtag Strategy
- Max 2-3 hashtags per tweet
- Branded: #STRAT #STRATblockchain
- Industry: #Crypto #Blockchain #DeFi #Web3
- Trending: Only if relevant

- Use polls weekly
- Retweet community content
