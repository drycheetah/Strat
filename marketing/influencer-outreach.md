## Influencer Outreach Process

### Step 1: Research & Qualification

**Criteria**:
- Follower count (tier appropriate)
- Engagement rate (minimum 2%)
- Content quality and authenticity
- Audience demographics (crypto-native)
- Past sponsored content (quality check)
- Reputation (no scam promotion history)

**Tools**:
- Social Blade (follower growth)
- Hype Auditor (engagement, fake followers)
- Manual review (content quality)

---

### Step 2: Initial Outreach

**Email Template** (Twitter/YouTube):

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity with STRAT Blockchain

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] from STRAT, a new high-performance blockchain that's making waves in the crypto space with our 100,000+ TPS and sub-second finality.

I've been following your content for a while - particularly [mention specific video/tweet] - and I think there's a great fit for a partnership.

STRAT has:
• Raised $20M from Sequoia, a16z, Pantera
• Launched mainnet with 1,000+ validators
• 50,000+ community members
• Full EVM compatibility

We're looking for authentic voices like yours to help spread the word to your [X] followers.

Would you be interested in discussing a partnership? We're flexible on format and open to creative ideas.

Looking forward to hearing from you!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Title]
STRAT Foundation

Website: strat.network
Twitter: @STRATblockchain
```

---

### Step 3: Negotiation

**Standard Terms**:
- Content type and format
- Publishing timeline
- Deliverables (video, tweets, stories, etc.)
- Usage rights (can STRAT repost?)
- Disclosure requirements (#ad, #sponsored)
- Payment terms (50% upfront, 50% on delivery)
- Exclusivity period (optional)

**Payment Methods**:
- USD via bank transfer/PayPal
- Crypto (USDC or STRAT tokens)
- Mix (50% USD, 50% STRAT)

**Contract Template**:
- Scope of work
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Payment terms
- Disclosure requirements
- Usage rights
- Termination clause

---

### Step 4: Content Creation

**Brand Guidelines**:
- Use official STRAT logo
- Accurate statistics and facts
- Balanced perspective (pros and cons)
- Disclosure of sponsorship
- No price predictions (unless data-backed)

**Review Process**:
1. Influencer creates draft
2. STRAT reviews for accuracy
3. Feedback and revisions
4. Final approval
5. Publishing

**Timeline**: 1-2 weeks from agreement to publishing

---

### Step 5: Promotion & Amplification

**When Influencer Posts**:
- Retweet/share immediately
- Engage in comments
- Pin to STRAT social media
- Feature in newsletter
- Add to website press page
- Track performance metrics

---

### Step 6: Performance Tracking

**Metrics to Track**:
- Views/impressions
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate (if link included)
- Conversions (website visits, wallet downloads)
- Sentiment (positive/negative comments)
- ROI (cost per acquisition)

**Tools**:
- YouTube Analytics (for video views)
- Twitter Analytics (for tweet impressions)
- Bitly links (for click tracking)
- Google Analytics (for website traffic)
- Sentiment analysis tools

## Red Flags & Avoid List

**Do NOT work with**:
- Scam promoters
- Pumpers/dumpers with bad reputation
- Fake follower accounts (>30% fake)
- Those who won't disclose sponsorship
- Controversial figures (unless strategic)
- Price manipulation promoters

**Due Diligence**:
- Check their past promoted projects
- Google "[Influencer name] scam"
- Review comment sentiment
- Ask for media kit and analytics

---

## Legal & Compliance

**Disclosure Requirements**:
- FTC guidelines (for US influencers)
- Clear and conspicuous disclosure
- Use #ad or #sponsored hashtag
- Verbal disclosure in videos

**Contract Must Include**:
- Scope of work
- Compensation
- Disclosure requirements
- Usage rights
- Liability limitations
- Termination clause

**Template**: Have legal counsel draft standard influencer agreement
