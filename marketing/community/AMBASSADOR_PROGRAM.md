# STRAT Ambassador Program

## Overview

The STRAT Ambassador Program recognizes and rewards community members who actively promote, support, and grow the STRAT ecosystem. Ambassadors serve as bridges between the core team and the broader community.

## Program Benefits

### Recognition
- Official STRAT Ambassador badge
- Featured on website and social media
- Direct communication channel with core team
- Early access to features and updates
- Ambassador-only Discord role

### Rewards
- Monthly STRAT allocation (based on contributions)
- Exclusive merchandise and swag
- Conference/event sponsorship
- Speaking opportunities
- Co-marketing support for projects

### Growth Opportunities
- Leadership development
- Technical training
- Networking with other ambassadors
- Portfolio building
- Career advancement connections

## Ambassador Responsibilities

### Content Creation (Choose 2-3)
- Write technical blog posts
- Create video tutorials
- Develop educational content
- Translate documentation
- Design graphics/infographics

### Community Engagement (Choose 2-3)
- Answer questions in Discord/Telegram
- Moderate community channels
- Organize local meetups
- Host AMAs or Twitter Spaces
- Welcome and onboard new members

### Development Support (Choose 1-2)
- Build example dApps
- Contribute to core code
- Write developer tools
- Create SDKs/libraries
- Improve documentation

### Advocacy (Choose 2-3)
- Represent STRAT at events
- Share updates on social media
- Engage with crypto community
- Write guest posts
- Create memes and viral content

## Tiers

### Bronze Ambassador
**Requirements:**
- 3 months community activity
- 5 quality contributions
- Active in Discord (weekly)
- Positive community reputation

**Benefits:**
- Ambassador badge
- 100 STRAT/month
- Early feature access
- Basic swag package

### Silver Ambassador
**Requirements:**
- 6 months as Bronze
- 15 quality contributions
- Organize 1 community event
- Help onboard 10+ new users

**Benefits:**
- Silver badge
- 250 STRAT/month
- Event sponsorship ($200)
- Premium swag package
- Featured on website

### Gold Ambassador
**Requirements:**
- 12 months as Silver
- 50 quality contributions
- Organize 3+ community events
- Lead major initiative
- Significant ecosystem impact

**Benefits:**
- Gold badge
- 500 STRAT/month
- Conference sponsorship ($1000)
- Custom swag
- Speaking opportunities
- Direct team collaboration

### Diamond Ambassador (Invite-Only)
**Requirements:**
- Exceptional long-term contributions
- Major ecosystem development
- International recognition
- Core team recommendation

**Benefits:**
- Diamond badge
- 1000+ STRAT/month
- Full conference sponsorship
- Advisory role
- Revenue sharing opportunities
- Governance participation

## Application Process

### 1. Eligibility Check
- Active in STRAT community for 3+ months
- Positive reputation
- Understanding of STRAT technology
- Commitment to program responsibilities

### 2. Application
Submit via: https://forms.stratblockchain.io/ambassador

**Required Information:**
- Name and location
- Social media profiles
- STRAT wallet address
- Community contributions (portfolio)
- Areas of interest
- Why you want to be an ambassador
- Time commitment (hours/week)

### 3. Review
- Team reviews applications monthly
- 1-2 week processing time
- Interview for Gold/Diamond tier
- Decision notification via email

### 4. Onboarding
- Welcome package
- Ambassador resources access
- Team introduction
- First month goals setting
- Badge and wallet setup

## Contribution Guidelines

### Quality Standards
- Original content (no plagiarism)
- Accurate information
- Professional presentation
- STRAT brand guidelines followed
- Value-adding to community

### Content Examples

**High Quality:**
- Detailed technical tutorial
- Original research/analysis
- Well-produced video
- Comprehensive guide
- Successful community event

**Medium Quality:**
- Social media thread
- Translation of existing content
- Community question answered
- Meme/graphic created
- Bug report with details

**Low Quality:**
- Generic retweet
- Low-effort post
- Copied content
- Spam promotion
- Unhelpful engagement

### Tracking Contributions

Use the Ambassador Portal:
https://ambassador.stratblockchain.io

**Log your contributions:**
1. Type (blog, video, event, etc.)
2. Link/proof
3. Date
4. Impact metrics (views, engagement)
5. Description

**Monthly Review:**
- Team reviews all contributions
- Assigns quality scores
- Calculates rewards
- Provides feedback
- Updates tier status

## Code of Conduct

### Required Behavior
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Help others genuinely
- Represent STRAT positively
- Follow community guidelines
- Disclose any conflicts of interest

### Prohibited Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination
- Spreading misinformation
- Pump-and-dump schemes
- Market manipulation
- Impersonating team members
- Sharing insider information
- Self-dealing or fraud

### Consequences
- Warning (first minor offense)
- Probation (repeated/serious)
- Demotion in tier
- Removal from program
- Ban from community (severe)

## FAQ

**Q: How much time is required?**
A: Minimum 5 hours/week for Bronze, more for higher tiers. Flexible schedule.

**Q: Can I be an ambassador while building on STRAT?**
A: Yes! Many ambassadors are also developers building dApps.

**Q: Are rewards in STRAT tokens?**
A: Yes, paid monthly in STRAT to your wallet address.

**Q: Can I lose ambassador status?**
A: Yes, if inactive for 2+ months or violate code of conduct.

**Q: Is there a limit on ambassadors?**
A: No hard limit, but we maintain quality standards.

**Q: Can I apply if I'm not a developer?**
A: Absolutely! We need creators, organizers, and advocates too.

**Q: How are contributions verified?**
A: You submit proof (links, screenshots), team reviews monthly.

**Q: Can I be anonymous?**
A: Partially. We need contact info, but can use pseudonym publicly.
