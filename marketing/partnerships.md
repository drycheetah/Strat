## Partnership Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Research & Qualification (2 weeks)

**For each potential partner**:
1. Research company and decision makers
2. Identify mutual benefits
3. Prepare custom pitch deck
4. Draft personalized outreach email

---

### Phase 2: Initial Contact (1-2 weeks)

**Outreach Channels**:
- Direct email to BD team
- LinkedIn connection with BD leads
- Introduction via mutual connections
- Conference/event meetups
- Twitter engagement

**Email Template**:
```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity: [PARTNER] × STRAT

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] from STRAT, a new high-performance blockchain that's
EVM-compatible with 100,000+ TPS and sub-second finality.

We've launched mainnet with [achievements] and are building strategic
partnerships to expand our ecosystem.

I believe there's a strong synergy between [PARTNER] and STRAT:

• [Mutual Benefit 1]
• [Mutual Benefit 2]
• [Mutual Benefit 3]

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to explore a potential partnership?

I've attached a brief overview of STRAT and the partnership opportunity.

Looking forward to connecting!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title]
STRAT Foundation

P.S. [Personalized note showing you've done research]
```

---

### Phase 3: Discovery Call (30 minutes)

**Agenda**:
1. Introductions (5 min)
2. STRAT overview (10 min)
3. Partnership vision (5 min)
4. [Partner] goals and needs (5 min)
5. Next steps (5 min)

**Goals**:
- Understand their priorities
- Present mutual benefits
- Identify decision makers
- Agree on follow-up

---

### Phase 4: Proposal (1 week)

**Custom Proposal Contents**:
1. Executive Summary
2. STRAT Overview
3. Partnership Vision
4. Technical Integration Plan
5. Go-to-Market Strategy
6. Success Metrics
7. Timeline & Milestones
8. Terms & Investment

---

### Phase 5: Negotiation (2-4 weeks)

**Key Points**:
- Win-win structure
- Clear deliverables
- Measurable outcomes
- Mutual investment
- Exclusivity considerations

---

### Phase 6: Execution (Ongoing)

**Partnership Success Factors**:
- Dedicated point person
- Regular check-ins
- Transparent communication
- Quick issue resolution
- Celebrate milestones
