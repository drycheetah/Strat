## Crisis Management Preparedness

### Potential Crises & Response Plans

**Security Breach**:
- Template: press-release.md (crisis announcement)
- Response time: <1 hour
- Communication: All channels simultaneously
- Transparency: Full disclosure with action plan

**Market Crash**:
- Focus on fundamentals, not price
- Reassure community with development updates
- Maintain consistent communication
- No panic, no FUD, just facts

**Negative Press**:
- Rapid response team activated
- Fact-based rebuttal prepared
- Transparent communication
- Community mobilization (if unfair attack)

**Community Conflict**:
- Active moderation
- Clear community guidelines
- De-escalation tactics
- Private resolution when possible

**Team Member Issue**:
- Prepared statement template
- Separation if needed
- Maintain project credibility
- Focus on project, not individuals
